### Commands

m!play or m!p
Search for the song you want to play

m!skip or m!s
Skips the current song

m!stop
Stops the queue

m!queue or m!q
Shows the current queue

m!np
Shows what is now playing

m!pause or m!p
Pauses the queue

m!resume or m!r
Continues playing the current queue

m!support
Support server

m!invite
Invite link for the bot

m!bugreport
Reports a bug

m!uptime
Shows the bots uptime

m!stats
Bot stats

m!site
Bots website
